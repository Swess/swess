Half-Lambert Shader

Graphics Tech Art

Created a fully customized lighting Uber shader for our current project at Canorous Interactive, supporting nearly all the same features as Unity’s main Lit shader. The Half-Lambert lighting term is utilized in various commercial games such as Team Fortress 2 and Valorant, and extensively described online. ScreenSpace Simulation

Tech Art Graphics VFX

This is a 2D fullscreen simulation VFX driven by the pixel channels’ values. From the initial snapshot, different channels and values are stored in texture buffers for the simulation to consume and modify. In the example video below, an outward velocity vector is added so that the displacement is directed toward the edges of the screen. Various controls are available for the simulation to achieve different results. For example, dissipation per color channel, viscosity, velocity direction, and intensity, among others.

Stylized Fog Shader

Home Project

GameJam

Small atmospheric and unique survival game made with Unity for the Global GameJam 2019. The theme of the GGJ 2019 was: What home means to you The prototype has been developed with a small team of 6 persons, including 2 programmers, 1 game designer & 3 artists. Our team, called JAMKIS, was part of the second season of JamNation. The game brings you in an ancestral adventure where you play the role of an Inuit tasked to finish the construction of your igloo on time while maintaining your family alive and warm.

Safeguard the realm

GameJam

A tower defense concept combined with an auto-battler in-game progression inspiration. This game project was executed as a team effort with 6 people in a course of Game Development at Concordia University. Safeguard The Realm! is a tower defense game where the right strategy is absolutely necessary in order to not lose. The objective of the game is to defend the castle against waves of enemies, by placing various types of defense towers on the grid map.
